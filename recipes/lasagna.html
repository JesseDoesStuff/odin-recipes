Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe has a 5-star rating on allrecipes.com
and is a fan-favorite in many households. It has a fantastic sauce
and a great blend of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
