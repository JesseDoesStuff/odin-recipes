Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

These light and fluffy pancakes have a 5-star review
on allrecipes.com.Try making these instead of using
store-bought mix the next time you make pancakes!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients in a seperate bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and cook the pancakes
    on a griddle until they're brown.
